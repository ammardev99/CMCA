I'm Ammar Shafiq and I'm excited to announce my cmca App project, In collaboration with Malik Saqib Faraz, a Civil Engineer.

𝐂𝐢𝐯𝐢𝐥 𝐌𝐚𝐭𝐞𝐫𝐢𝐚𝐥𝐬 𝐂𝐨𝐬𝐭 𝐀𝐧𝐚𝐥𝐲𝐬𝐢𝐬
This innovative software will enable fast and efficient creation of estimates for various aspects of civil engineering projects.

- Design Estimate
- Foundation Estimate
- Masonry Estimate
- Roof Estimate
- Electrical Estimate
- Plumbing Estimate
- Mechanical Estimate
- Steel Estimate
- Finishing Estimate
- Furniture Estimate

Our vision is to expand this software to build a comprehensive civil community platform, connecting civil engineers, contractors, consultants, construction managers, supervisors, site inspectors, and more.

- Versions
1.0.1 Estimate Options
1.0.2 Estimate Forms & Cost calculations
1.0.3 Show Result and code optimization
